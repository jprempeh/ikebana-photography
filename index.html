About

Welcome to Serene Ikebana Design photo gallery. I make creative Ikebana arrangements for museums, corporate receptions, churches, parties and events. I hold workshops that focus on the mindful and relaxing nature of the Ikebana making process. I also teach therapeutic Ikebana classes to handicapped young adults in the community.
